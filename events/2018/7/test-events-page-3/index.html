WAV.fest

Sat, Jul 14, 9 PM
Substation
645 NW 45th St, Seattle, WA 98107
$10 & 21+

The first WAV.fest event brought lots of amazing talent from across the country to share the stage at Substation in Seattle for a killer throwdown of beats and grooves. WAV.fest became our annual summer show, with an emphasis on bringing together the local scene with the broader national, and sometimes international, one!
