Postponed Workshop

by Nikola on 2020-05-04


We had planned a chill event with live music and visuals and a chiptune workshop, slated for March 22nd at Capitol Hill's hip bar, Vermillion. Due to the coronavirus, we had to cancel this event, public health and safety should always be a #1 priority. However, I wanted a place to share the killer flyer art by Emmy Toyonaga, and to reaffirm our commitment to help foster the local scene! As soon as it is safe to host physical workshop-style events like this, we will be back at it for sure!
