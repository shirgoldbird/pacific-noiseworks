Website Launch!

by Nikola on 2020-05-04

Welcome to pacificnoiseworks.com! We just launched this site as a way to both keep together info related to our past events, as well as to host info for upcoming events!

Missed out on a WAV.fest event and want to check out some pictures? We'll have flickr embeds of all shows where we had photographers! Want to revisit a HEAT.wav event and check out its playlist? We'll have soundcloud embeds here for that! Want to check in on the state of Pacific Noise Works? Blog posts! This site aims to be a centralized place to document the growing chiptune/hardware scene in the Seattle area.

As always, feel free to link up with us on your favorite social media as well - links to Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and more can be found at the bottom of the page.
